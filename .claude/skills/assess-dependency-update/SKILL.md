---
name: assess-dependency-update
description: Review the impact of a dependency update on your project, and highlight any breaking changes or compatibility issues.
---

1. **Fetch the release notes** for the proposed version of the dependency update.
2. **Summarize**: Recap the release notes, focusing on major and minor features. Include a link to the changelog or release notes.
3. **Identify our usage**: Where in our codebase do we use this dependency, and how might the update affect us?
4. **Identify compatibility issues**: Are there any breaking changes or deprecations that could impact our project?
